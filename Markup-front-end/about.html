Meet Markup
Your Automated Answer Evaluation Partner. Powered by Pixelparse, GraphoMind, and Riddler, we're revolutionizing assessment with cutting-edge AI solutions.
< Our Vision >
Transform the educational assessment landscape by harnessing the power of advanced AI models driven by deep learning neural networks. We aim to provide educators with innovative tools and methodologies that streamline the assessment process, enhance efficiency, and improve accuracy.
< Our Approach >
Markup employs a transformative two-phase methodology to revolutionize educational assessment. In the initial phase, our platform digitizes answer sheets, identifies the need for figures, and creates comprehensive answer keys. In the second phase, we integrate individual models, compare answers and figures with the answer key, and develop a sophisticated marking system along with a user-friendly front end.
